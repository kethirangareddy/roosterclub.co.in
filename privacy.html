Privacy Policy

Effective: 2 June 2025  •  Last updated: 2 June 2025

Rooster Club ("we", "our", "us") operates the Rooster Club mobile application (the "App"), a marketplace for poultry breeders, buyers and enthusiasts in India. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. By using the App you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number — used to create your account and verify your identity via SMS OTP (delivered by Twilio).
  • Profile information — name, username, location (state/district/area), breed specialty, bio, profile photo (all optional, provided by you).
  • Approximate location — used to show listings and buyers near you. We do not continuously track your location.
  • Listings & content — photos, short videos, descriptions, prices and tags you upload (stored on Cloudinary CDN).
  • Chat messages — text exchanged with other users, stored to allow conversation history.
  • Push notification token — issued by Expo / Firebase, used only to deliver in-app notifications.
  • Device information — device model, OS version, app version, language preference, for diagnostics.
  • Usage data — basic, anonymised events (screens viewed, button taps) for product improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and prevent fraud.
  • Show you listings near your area and translate descriptions into your preferred language.
  • Enable real-time chat between buyers and sellers.
  • Send push notifications about messages, listing approvals, and account activity.
  • Moderate the marketplace (reviewing reports, blocking abusive users).
  • Improve App performance and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Twilio — sends SMS OTPs to verify your phone number. Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary — stores and delivers your listing photos and videos. Cloudinary Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud Translation — translates listing descriptions on demand. Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo / Firebase — delivers push notifications.

4. Data Sharing & Visibility

  • Your listings (photos, descriptions, price, area) are public and visible to all App users.
  • Your name, username, area, breed specialty and seller rating are visible on your public profile.
  • Your phone number is hidden by default. It is only shown to other users if you explicitly toggle "Show phone publicly" in your profile.
  • Your chat messages are private and visible only to the participants of that conversation.
  • We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or any third party.
  • We may disclose data when required by Indian law (court orders, lawful government requests).

5. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Passwords (admin only) are hashed with bcrypt. Backend servers are hosted on secured cloud infrastructure with restricted access. We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active.
  • Listings expire automatically 30 days after posting (unless renewed) but the listing data is kept for moderation history.
  • If you delete your account, your profile, listings and chat messages are removed within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update inaccurate information from inside the App.
  • Delete your account and associated data (Settings → Account → Delete account, or email us).
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time from device settings or in-app preferences.
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing privacy@roosterclub.co.in.

8. Children's Privacy

Rooster Club is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

9. Animal Welfare & Legal Trading

Rooster Club is a marketplace for legal poultry trading only — breeders, hens, fighters (for breeding/exhibition purposes), chicks and eggs. Illegal activities including cockfighting for monetary gain, animal cruelty, or sale of protected species are strictly prohibited and will result in account termination and may be reported to local authorities. Users must comply with the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 and all applicable state/central laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
