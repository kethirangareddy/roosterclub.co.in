Delete your account

Rooster Club · account & data deletion

You can permanently delete your Rooster Club account and personal data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

Open Rooster Club and go to Profile → ··· menu → Delete account. You'll confirm, and your account is removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can't use the app, email support@roosterclub.co.in from any address with the phone number on your account. We will verify it's you and delete your account.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your:

  • Profile and personal details (name, phone number, location)
  • Listings, posts, photos and videos
  • Chats and messages
  • KYC documents (ID photo and selfie)
  • Follows, reviews, badges and referral records

What may be retained

We keep only limited records that we are required to retain for legal, fraud-prevention, or dispute reasons, and only for as long as necessary. Shared transaction records (such as a deal receipt) may remain visible to the other party to that deal.

How long it takes

In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are processed after we verify your identity, typically within a few days.

Questions

Contact support@roosterclub.co.in. See also our Privacy Policy.
