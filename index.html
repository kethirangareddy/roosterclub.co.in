Why Rooster Club

Built for trust, not chaos

Buying birds over scattered WhatsApp groups is risky — fake sellers, no records, no recourse. Rooster Club brings the whole trade into one safe, organised place where sellers are verified and every deal is on record.

🛡️

Verified sellers

Sellers complete KYC to earn a verified badge, so you know exactly who you're dealing with before you travel or pay a rupee.

🧾

Deal receipts

Confirm each deal with a digital receipt between buyer and seller — a clear, mutual record that builds reputation and ends disputes.

🏆

Honest-seller leaderboard

Genuine sellers rise to the top of their mandal, district and state — recognition earned through real sales and reviews, never paid ads.
