Everything in one app

Every feature, built for the trade

Rooster Club replaces scattered WhatsApp groups with one organised, trusted home for buying, selling, learning and protecting your birds. Here's everything it does.

Buy & sell

The marketplace

🐓

Listings by breed, type & price

Post and discover birds across every category — fighters, breeders, layers, show birds, eggs and chicks. Each listing carries photos, breed, age, weight, colour, price and location, so buyers know exactly what they're getting before they message.

⚡

Live auctions

Run or join real-time auctions with live bidding, host approval of bidders, and a countdown timer. The seller finalises the winning bid — bringing the energy of a real auction yard online, with a clear record of who won.

📍

Listings near you

GPS-based "near me" filtering surfaces birds and sellers closest to you first, right down to your district and mandal — because most poultry deals happen locally and in person.

🛒

Pre-book eggs & chicks

Reserve hatching eggs and day-old chicks before they're ready. Buyers place a pre-booking; sellers manage their waiting list and notify everyone the moment stock is available.

🌾

Live feed & supplies

Source feed, medicine and supplies from sellers near you — listed and searchable inside the same app, so you never leave to find what your flock needs.

Trust & safety

How we keep deals honest

🛡️

KYC-verified sellers

Sellers can complete identity verification to earn a verified badge. ID documents are reviewed privately and then deleted — buyers simply see a trusted badge, so they know who they're dealing with before they travel or pay.

🧾

Deal receipts

When a deal closes, buyer and seller confirm a digital receipt together. It's a mutual, tamper-resistant record that builds each person's reputation and settles "he said / she said" disputes.

⭐

Ratings & reviews

Buyers rate sellers after a deal. Honest sellers accumulate genuine reviews that travel with their profile — reputation you can actually see.

🏆

Honest-seller leaderboard

Sellers rise on a leaderboard for their mandal, district and state based on real sales and reviews — never paid placement. The best sellers earn visibility by being trustworthy.

🎖️

Seller badges & tiers

Bronze, Silver, Gold and founding-member badges reward consistent, trusted sellers with recognition and a ranking boost — so good actors stand out in search.

🚩

Report & block

Anyone can report a suspicious listing or user, and block people they don't want to hear from. Our team can remove content and ban bad actors.

Community & care

More than a marketplace

💬

Chat & voice notes

Message any buyer or seller directly, send photos and record voice notes — perfect for quick questions when typing in your language is slower than speaking.

📰

Community feed

Follow other breeders and scroll a feed of birds, updates and posts from your community — a social home for the people who love this hobby.

🩺

Vet directory

Find poultry veterinarians and contact them directly the moment your flock needs help — no more hunting for a number when a bird falls sick.

⚠️

Disease alerts

Get warned about disease outbreaks reported in your district so you can quarantine, vaccinate and protect your birds before trouble reaches your pen.

🎁

Invite & earn

Invite fellow breeders and earn feature credits you can use to boost your own listings to the top — growth driven by the community itself.

Your experience

Designed for every Indian keeper

🌐

Six languages, fully translated

Every screen and button works in Telugu, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Malayalam and English — not just the menus. The app opens in English and instantly switches to whatever you choose.

📶

Works on patchy networks

Your feed is cached so the app still opens and shows birds even when the signal drops — built for rural and small-town connectivity.

🔔

Helpful notifications

Get notified about new messages, bids, pre-booking availability and disease alerts in your area — the things that actually matter, nothing spammy.

₹

Free & commission-free

Listing and using Rooster Club is free, and we take zero commission on deals. Every rupee of every sale stays between buyer and seller.
