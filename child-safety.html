Child Safety Standards

Effective 20 June 2026

Rooster Club ("we", "us") has a zero-tolerance approach to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Rooster Club is an adults-only (18+) marketplace and community for poultry and gamefowl. It is not designed for, directed to, or intended to be used by children. This page describes the standards we maintain to prevent, detect, and respond to child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any conduct that endangers children, in line with Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy and applicable law.

1. Our commitment

We prohibit child sexual abuse material and any content or behaviour that sexualises, exploits, endangers, or harms children. This applies to every part of the app — listings, photos, videos, posts, profiles, chat, voice messages, and any other user-generated content. Accounts that create, share, request, or facilitate such content or conduct are removed and reported to the appropriate authorities.

2. Prohibited conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on Rooster Club: child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form; sexualisation of minors; grooming, solicitation, or sextortion of a child; trafficking or endangerment of a child; and sharing links or contact details intended to distribute such material. We also prohibit the use of our service to make contact with a child for any abusive or exploitative purpose.

3. Prevention and moderation

We reduce risk through several measures: the app is restricted to adults (18+); sellers can complete KYC verification; and every piece of user-generated content can be reported by users and reviewed by our moderation team. Users can block other users, and our administrators can remove content and permanently ban offending accounts. We act on reports promptly and remove violating content as quickly as we are able.

4. Reporting and response

Anyone can report suspected CSAE in two ways: using the in-app Report and Block tools available on listings, profiles, and chats, or by emailing our child-safety contact below. Reports of CSAE are treated as the highest priority. When we identify or confirm CSAE, we remove the content, disable the responsible account, preserve relevant records as required, and report to the relevant authorities — including law enforcement and, where applicable, the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and Indian authorities under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act and the Information Technology Act.

5. Legal compliance

We comply with applicable child-safety laws in India and the other regions where the app is available, and with Google Play's Child Safety Standards and CSAE policies. We cooperate with law enforcement and recognised child-protection organisations in good faith.

6. Child-safety point of contact

To report child sexual abuse or exploitation, or for any child-safety question, contact our designated point of contact: childsafety@roosterclub.co.in. For general support you can also reach us at support@roosterclub.co.in.

7. Updates

We review and update these standards as our service and the applicable requirements evolve.
